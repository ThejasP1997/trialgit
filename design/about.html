Car Spa builds and operates car care supercenters all over Bangalore. It offers exterior soft cloth car wash, full-service car wash, car detailing, lube, emissions/state inspection, and headlight restoration services. The company also operates gasoline filling stations with convenience stores. Car Spa was incorporated in 1998 and is based in Dallas, Texas.
